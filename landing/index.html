Security

Built paranoid.
By design.

Security isn't a feature checkbox. Every architectural decision was made with credential safety as the top priority.

AES-256-GCM Authenticated Encryption

Every vault entry is independently encrypted with a unique nonce. GCM mode provides both confidentiality and data integrity — ciphertext tampering is detected.

Argon2id Memory-Hard KDF

Your master password is never stored. It's stretched to a 256-bit key via Argon2id (64 MiB, 3 passes, 4 threads) — the OWASP 2024 "High Security" standard, resistant to GPU and ASIC brute-force.

Zero Plaintext — Vault-Only, Local-Only

Credentials are decrypted in memory only when actively used, immediately scrubbed after. Nothing is written to disk unencrypted. Zero network egress of secrets.

Auto-Lock & Session Timeout

Configurable inactivity timer re-encrypts the vault and requires master password re-entry. Walk away from your desk with confidence.
